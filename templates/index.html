Maryland Water and Science Administration Inspections

Who's polluting Maryland's water, and who is Maryland penalizing for it?

Photo: Chesapeake Bay Program | Flickr

Inspectors from the Maryland Water and Science Administration visit sites that discharge wastewater to determine their compliance with their National Pollutant Discharge Elimination System (NPDES) permits and report the results of these inspections to the Maryland Department of the Environment each year.

WSA publishes its compliance inspections here and its enforcement actions here. MDE also releases an Annual Enforcement & Compliance Report including WSA reported data.

But because inspection and enforcement data are published separately, state reporting doesn't make it easy to trace inspections and enforcement actions for individual sites.
