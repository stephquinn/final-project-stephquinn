Maryland Water and Science Administration Inspections

Inspectors from the WSA's field offices visit sites that discharge wastewater to determine their compliance with their National Pollutant Discharge Elimination System permits and report the results of these inspections to the Maryland Department of the Environment each year.

WSA publishes its compliance inspections here and its enforcement actions here. MDE also releases an Annual Enforcement & Compliance Report including WSA reported data.
